Our Mission

At FilmDiary, our mission is to create the ultimate movie database, providing comprehensive listings of every movie ever made from every corner of the globe. We aim to be the go-to resource for movie enthusiasts, researchers, and casual viewers alike, offering detailed information, historical context, and up-to-date listings.

Our Story

The idea for FilmDiary was born out of a simple frustration: the lack of a single, reliable source for complete movie listings. Existing platforms were often incomplete or region-specific. We decided to create an app that fills this gap, providing an inclusive, accurate, and user-friendly movie database.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Database:

    Our app lists movies from the inception of cinema to the latest releases, covering every genre, era, and country.

  • Detailed Information:

    Each movie entry includes cast and crew details, synopsis, release dates, trivia, and user reviews.

  • User-Friendly Interface:

    Easy navigation and powerful search features allow users to find movies quickly and efficiently.

  • Time Tracking Feature:

    After watching a movie or a series, simply log your entry. Our app will automatically find the duration and keep track of your watch history. You can see how much time you’ve spent watching movies and series, helping you manage your viewing habits. The app calculates and displays the total time spent watching movies and series over a week or a month, based on your preference.

  • Community Engagement:

    Users can rate movies, leave reviews, and participate in discussions.

Special Features

  • Personalized Recommendations:

    Our platform offers personalized movie recommendations based on users' viewing history and preferences, ensuring that they discover new films they'll love.

  • Interactive Quizzes:

    Test your movie knowledge with our interactive quizzes covering various genres, directors, and film trivia.

  • Global Chat Groups:

    Engage with movie enthusiasts from different countries by joining chat groups based on your interests. Discover new perspectives, share recommendations, and connect with fellow cinephiles around the world. Additionally, users can create their own chat groups to discuss specific movies or series they're passionate about.
